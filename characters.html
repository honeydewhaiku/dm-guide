Character Creation

1. You may choose any race or class published in D&D 5e.

2. You may choose a homebrew race or class, if you seek permission from the DM first.

3. As I will not oversee your character creation, either use the standard spread of scores or customize your scores using the table on page 13 of the Player's Handbook.

4. Begin with 64,000 experience points.

5. Your character must start at Level 10.

6. Select either a +2 magic weapon (your choice) OR +2 Mithral armor (any medium or heavy armor except hide, no disadvantage on stealth checks, no strength requirement).

7. Select either goggles of night (grants darkvision 60 ft) OR gloves of thievery (+5 bonus to lockpicking and sleight of hand) OR ring of mind shielding (immune to others reading your thoughts).

8. Start with 3 Potions of Healing and 200 GP. You may spend the GP on your starting items.
