Gods

Many people in this world have forgotten the gods and become atheistic. Those who still believe worship one of four deities.


Amaterasu, goddess of the sun. Lawful good.
The goddess of the sun and ruler of the heavens. The most popularly worshipped deity.

Tsukiyomi, god of the moon. Lawful neutral.
The god of the moon. He separated himself from Amaterasu, moving to the opposite end of the sky, after they had a falling out. Amaterasu had asked him to represent her at a feast hosted by the goddess of food. However, Tsukiyomi was disgusted with the manner in which the goddess prepared food (by spitting it out of her mouth), and killed her. Ashamed, Amaterasu banished him from the heavens.

Susanoo, god of storms. Chaotic good.
The god of the sea and storms, he killed the eight-headed dragon Orochi using his wit: he placed eight barrels of sake as a trap for each of the eight heads. When Orochi became drunk, he slayed the beast with his giant sword.

Orochi, 8-headed dragon. Neutral evil.
An enormous, red-eyed dragon with eight heads and trees growing from his ancient back, Orochi requires the yearly sacrifice of a young girl as offering from each village he rules over. If the villagers refuse to present him with such a sacrifice, he ruthlessly destroys the village. Many years ago he was slayed by Susanoo, but he has since been revived by dark magic.
